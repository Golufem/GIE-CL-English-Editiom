CL&GIE

Carbon Literacy & Green Innovation Entrepreneurship

This is an Instructional Learning Material designed to give you the knowledge, motivation and make you feel more empowered to act on climate change, at school and in own life.

    Computer Aided Translation Teaching System for Carbon Literacy & Green Innovation Entrepreneurship Modules

  • 1 CL Module 1 Intro, Module 2 Basic Definitions, Module 3 Greenhouse Effect, Module 4 Latest Projection.
  • 2 GIE Module 1 Business Concepts, Module 2 Business Development Fundamental, Module 3 SDT, Business Case Studies, Business Development Practices.

Learning Objectives

Objective 1: To identify and explain key principles of Carbon and its resourcefulness.

Objective 2: To understand and be able to utilize an appropriate discourse on sustainability and the green business movement.

What is CL?

An awareness of the carbon cost and impacts of everyday activities and the ability and motivation to reduce emissions, on an individual, community and organizational basis.

GIE Business Concept?

To identify and explain key business concepts in green business movement. To understand and be able to utilize an appropriate discourse on sustainability and the green business movement.
